Global Zeal Inc.

Global Zeal is a Cebu-based software development firm specializing in Node.JS Development that combines industry-leading web application design expertise with proven business acumen to transform the way our clients do business.

History

For decades, there have been companies that specialize in outsourcing software development resources, and they’ve done a good job. However, the founders of Global Zeal envisioned doing things differently... Global Zeal, a division of one of the largest professional-development companies in the US, training over 1.3 million professionals at 25,000 institutions per year took a new approach. At Global Zeal we believe you ought to be involved in the decisions. From the start, you’ll have the option to interview and build your ‘Dream Team’ but it doesn’t stop there. You will also have access to communicate with your team nearly whenever you want either via phone, video chat or in person, we have the solutions to make it happen.
